About

Welcome to r.eci.pe - the Coder's Kitchen,

First off I should say this is not a blog - it's my recipe book. You won't find anecdotes or long winded stories here.

I'm gradually collating and digitising my favourite recipes, mostly to share with my family, but also so I can find and work on them more easily. Over time I will also probably add some of my more "experimental" efforts - like the Oak Smoked Sour Dough I'm currently playing with.

If you enjoy any of the recipes here, feel free to share.

Cheers,
